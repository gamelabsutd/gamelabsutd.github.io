Privacy Policy for Project Athena

Last Updated: 31 October 2023

SUTD Game Lab ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our application, Project Athena (the "App").

By using the App, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

We may collect and store personal information that you provide directly to us, including but not limited to:

a)      Name

b)     Email address

c)      Phone number

d)     User-generated content (e.g., photos or videos)

b. Device Information

When you use the App, we may collect certain information about your mobile device, including:

a)      Device type

b)     Operating system

c)      Unique device identifiers

d)     IP address

c. Location Information

The App may collect precise location information from your device when you enable GPS and location services. We use this information to provide location-based features and services.

d. Camera and Microphone Access

The App may request access to your device's camera and microphone. We use these permissions to enable features such as taking photos, recording videos, or using voice commands within the App.

 

2. How We Use Your Information

We may use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

a)      To provide, maintain, and improve the App's functionality and user experience.

b)     To respond to your requests, comments, or questions.

c)      To send you updates, notifications, and promotional messages.

d)     To analyze usage patterns and trends to enhance our services.

e)     To comply with legal obligations or protect our rights and the rights of others.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your personal information with third parties in the following situations:

a)      With your consent.

b)     With service providers and partners who assist us in delivering the App's features and services.

c)      When required by law or in response to legal requests.

d)     In connection with a merger, sale, or acquisition of all or a part of our company.

e)     To protect the rights, privacy, safety, or property of us, our users, or the public.

4. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Choices

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. You can also opt-out of receiving promotional communications from us. To exercise your rights or opt-out, please contact us at gamelab@sutd.du.sg

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated policy will be posted on this page with the revised "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at gamelab@sutd.edu.sg.
